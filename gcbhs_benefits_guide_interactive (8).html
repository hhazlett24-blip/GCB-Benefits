Life & AD&D Insurance (Mutual of Omaha)

▼

Basic life insurance and accidental death & dismemberment coverage provided by GCBHS at no cost to you.

Basic Life Coverage: $25,000 (employer-paid)
Basic AD&D Coverage: $25,000 (employer-paid)

Optional Supplemental Life Insurance:

Employee Supplemental Life: Available in $10,000 increments up to $200,000 | Guaranteed issue: $100,000 (no medical questions) | Evidence of insurability required above $100,000
Spouse Life: Available in $5,000 increments up to $100,000 (not to exceed 50% of employee coverage) | Guaranteed issue: $30,000
Child Life: $10,000 coverage per child (age 15 days to 26 years) | Guaranteed issue - no medical questions

Important: Supplemental life insurance premiums are based on age and increase every 5 years. Rates available upon request from HR.

Eligible: Full-time & Part-time (≥30 hrs/week)

Short-Term Disability (Mutual of Omaha)

▼

Income protection if you're unable to work due to illness or injury.

Coverage: 60% of weekly earnings up to $1,500 per week maximum
Elimination Period: 7 days for accident | 7 days for illness
Benefit Duration: Up to 13 weeks
Pre-existing Condition: 3/12 limitation applies (treatment in past 3 months, limitation for first 12 months of coverage)
Cost: Employer-paid benefit (no cost to employee)
Eligible: Full-time (≥40 hrs/week)

Long-Term Disability (Mutual of Omaha)

▼

Extended income protection for disabilities lasting beyond short-term coverage.

Coverage: 60% of monthly earnings up to $7,500 per month maximum
Elimination Period: 90 days
Benefit Duration: To age 65 (or Social Security Normal Retirement Age)
Own Occupation Period: 24 months (then any occupation)
Pre-existing Condition: 3/12 limitation applies
Cost: Employer-paid benefit (no cost to employee)
Eligible: Full-time (≥40 hrs/week)

Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

▼

Confidential counseling and support services for you and your family members at no cost.

Counseling Sessions: Up to 3 sessions per issue, per person, per year
Services Available: Personal counseling, work/life balance, legal consultation, financial guidance, family issues, stress management
Availability: 24/7 access by phone
Confidentiality: 100% confidential - your employer will not know you used the service
Cost: FREE - employer-paid benefit
Eligible: All employees and their household members

403(b) Retirement Plan (Empower)

▼

Build your retirement savings with employer contributions and tax advantages.

Employer Contribution:

Full-time Staff (≥40 hrs/week): 4% of gross pay regardless of employee contribution
Part-time Staff (30-39 hrs/week): 2% of gross pay regardless of employee contribution
Vesting Schedule: 20% per year | 100% vested after 5 years

Employee Voluntary Contributions:

2025 Contribution Limits: $23,000 annual limit (under age 50) | $30,500 with catch-up (age 50+)
Contribution Type: Pre-tax or Roth (after-tax) options available

Account Access:

Provider: Empower Retirement
Normal Retirement Age: 65
Early Retirement: Age 55 (if employed on that date)

Important: Distributions before age 59½ may be subject to 10% penalty tax. Required minimum distributions begin at age 72 (or retirement, if later).

Eligible: Full-time & Part-time (≥30 hrs/week) after 1 year service

Health Savings Account (HSA)

▼

Tax-advantaged savings account for medical expenses (available only with High Deductible Health Plan).

2025 Contribution Limits: $4,300 individual / $8,550 family | Additional $1,000 catch-up (age 55+)
Triple Tax Advantage: Tax-deductible contributions | Tax-free growth | Tax-free withdrawals for qualified medical expenses
Rollover: Unused funds roll over year to year - no "use it or lose it"
Portability: Account is yours to keep even if you change employers
Qualified Expenses: Medical, dental, vision, prescriptions, and more
Eligible: Employees enrolled in High Deductible Health Plan

Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)

▼

Pre-tax accounts to pay for eligible healthcare and dependent care expenses.

Healthcare FSA:

2025 Maximum: $3,300 per year
Eligible Expenses: Copays, deductibles, prescriptions, dental, vision, and other qualified medical expenses
Grace Period: 2.5 months after plan year end to incur expenses
Carryover: Up to $660 can be carried over to next year

Dependent Care FSA:

2025 Maximum: $5,000 per year (married filing jointly) | $2,500 (married filing separately)
Eligible Expenses: Daycare, preschool, before/after school programs, adult dependent care

How to File Claims:

Provider: Custom Design Benefits
Online Portal: www.CustomDesignBenefits.com → Member Portal → Custom Flex
Contact: 513.598.2929 (local) | 800.598.2929 (toll-free)

FSA Card: Nearly 80% of FSA card transactions are automatically approved. Keep receipts but do not send unless requested.

Eligible: Full-time & Part-time (≥30 hrs/week)

Paid Time Off (PTO)

▼

Flexible paid time off for vacation, personal time, and illness.

Annual PTO Accrual:

Years of Service Annual PTO Days Accrual per Pay Period
0-2 years 15 days 4.62 hours (bi-weekly)
3-5 years 20 days 6.15 hours (bi-weekly)
6-10 years 25 days 7.69 hours (bi-weekly)
11+ years 30 days 9.23 hours (bi-weekly)
Accrual Start: PTO begins accruing from your first day of employment
Usage: May use PTO after 90 days of employment
Maximum Accrual: 240 hours (30 days) | Accrual stops when maximum is reached
Payout at Termination: Unused PTO paid out upon separation (voluntary or involuntary)
Eligible: Full-time (≥40 hrs/week)

Family & Medical Leave (FMLA)

▼

Job-protected leave for family and medical reasons.

Duration: Up to 12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave in a 12-month period
Qualifying Reasons: Birth/adoption of child, serious health condition, care for family member with serious health condition
Eligibility Requirements: Employed for at least 12 months | Worked at least 1,250 hours in past 12 months
Benefits During Leave: Health insurance continues | Must use available PTO first
Job Protection: Return to same or equivalent position with same pay and benefits
Eligible: Staff meeting FMLA requirements

Parental Leave

▼

Additional paid leave for new parents to bond with their child.

Duration: 2 weeks (80 hours) of paid parental leave
Qualifying Events: Birth, adoption, or foster care placement of a child
Usage Window: Must be used within 12 months of child's arrival
Can be combined with: FMLA, PTO, and other applicable leave for extended time off
Eligible: Full-time staff with 1+ year of service

Bereavement Leave

▼

Paid time off following the death of a family member.

Duration: Up to 3 days paid leave
Covered Relationships: Spouse, domestic partner, child, parent, sibling, grandparent, grandchild, in-laws
Additional Time: Additional unpaid time or PTO may be available with supervisor approval
Eligible: All Staff

Jury Duty Leave

▼

Paid leave when summoned for jury duty.

Duration: Paid leave for the duration of jury service
Required Notice: Provide copy of jury summons to supervisor as soon as received
Jury Pay: You may keep any jury duty compensation received from the court
Eligible: Full-time Staff
